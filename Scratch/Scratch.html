Popular Music Genre Shifts

Resume





Here's a concise list highlighting some of the most popular music genres and artists over the last couple of decades:

  1. 70s
    • Rock and Disco Fusion: Bands like Bee Gees and ABBA dominated the disco scene.
    • Classic Rock: Led Zeppelin, The Rolling Stones, and Pink Floyd continued their influence.
  2. 80s
    • Pop and Synth-Pop: Madonna, Michael Jackson, and Prince defined the pop landscape.
    • Rock and Heavy Metal: Bon Jovi, Guns N' Roses, and Metallica gained massive popularity.
    • Hip-hop Emergence: The genre started to take shape with acts like Run-DMC and Public Enemy.
  3. 90s
    • Grunge and Alternative Rock: Nirvana, Pearl Jam, and Soundgarden reshaped the rock scene.
    • Pop and R&B: Boy bands (Backstreet Boys, *NSYNC) and divas (Whitney Houston, Mariah Carey) thrived.
    • Hip-hop Golden Era: Tupac Shakur, The Notorious B.I.G., and Wu-Tang Clan left an indelible mark.
  4. 00s
    • Pop and Hip-hop: Britney Spears, Eminem, and Beyonc√© dominated.
    • Indie and Alternative Rock: The Strokes, Arctic Monkeys, and The White Stripes rose to prominence.
    • EDM Rise: Avicii, Calvin Harris, and David Guetta led the electronic dance music wave.
  5. 10s
    • K-pop Explosion: BTS, BLACKPINK, and EXO gained global recognition.
    • Hip-hop Dominance: Drake, Kendrick Lamar, and Cardi B shaped the genre.
    • Latin Takeover: Luis Fonsi, J Balvin, and Bad Bunny propelled Latin music worldwide.
